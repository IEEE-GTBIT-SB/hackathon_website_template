CRYPTO

Stock markets and derivatives are now a thing of the past. The progress Cryptocurrencies have seen in the past decade is beyond comprehension, while also being one of the main components of Web3, and DeFi. Crypto has taken the world by storm and if you want to do the same by taking the judges with a storm you must come up with the most thought provoking business ideas for this track, which is surely Up and Coming globally.

HEALTH MANAGEMENT

The Ideathon's focus is "Health management." We need your input on the overall leadership and management of both public and private healthcare organizations to improve the existing technology in the field.

TRAVEL/TOURISM

We all travel and have been tourists, perhaps many times in our life. Have you ever pondered about what travel and tourism are, despite their widespread popularity? Well, this Ideathon attempts to make you explore these concepts and share your findings with us about the new ideas you might discover on this track for the betterment of the industry.

SUSTAINABLE CONSUMPTION AND PRODUCTION

Sustainable Consumption and Production is about doing more and better with less. “Evolution of the Sustainable Consumption and Production” has been a theme at the UN and through this ideathon, we are now bringing it to you guys as well. The first production line was made in the 20th century and is still widely used, living in the 21st century, don't you think we should build something more apt for our generation.

MISCELLANEOUS

But why not allow everyone to experience that blended reality via our ideathons? Drop us a line with your thoughts on any interesting factoids you have about any idea that doesnt match with our current tracks.

Special Tracks

Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the defense against cyberthreats for systems connected to the internet, including their data, software, and hardware. Organizations use various techniques to prevent unauthorized access to data centers and other computerized systems. Risk management along with decision-makers who identify opportunities that go beyond preventing data breaches may begin to realize the full potential and benefits that cyber security can provide.

Edtech

Edtech is the integration of education, technology, and business. It refers to technological implementations in the classroom. In the pandemic era Edtech has become the new frontier of education.The ability to engage students is one of educational technology's greatest gifts, and thanks to its dynamic nature, it may completely alter how pupils learn in the classroom. Can you use the newest technologies to take on the toughest difficulties in education?

Agritech

By 2050, the global population is expected to increase by 30%, meaning there will be around 10 billion people to feed. The answer to the needs of modern agriculture is agritech, which blends agriculture with technology. From automated irrigation and software for predicting pests, to improved production - agritech is moving farming into the future. Get into agritech and leave a mark from the inside out.

Fintech

Fintech is short for financial technology. It refers to the use of technology to streamline and speed up financial services and transactions. FinTech businesses no longer offer "one size fits all" fixes. Instead, they offer specialized services that cater to a particular financial need, frequently for a lot less money than traditional financial service providers.
