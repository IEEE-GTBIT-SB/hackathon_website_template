About Us

IEEE GTBIT Student Branch is one of the most proactive student branches in the Delhi Section. With an abundance of outstanding talent from different domains, our student branch focuses on providing opportunities to those who wish to make a mark in their fields. The community is composed of like-minded volunteers and fosters the ideology of creating an impact. In the past few years, the volunteers of this student branch have showcased their mettle and won numerous laurels at sectional, national, and regional levels.
